Step 3 · Live sheet

Orders land in one live sheet your team already uses

Baztit syncs each closed sale from AI chat into a single spreadsheet: customer, line items, payment, and fulfillment status stay on one row. Ops sees the queue at a glance—no re-typing into another CRM. From here, Ship is the handoff that tells the system (and carriers) the parcel is going out.

Step 4 · Carrier

From ship click to tracking—without leaving the row

After Ship package, Baztit creates a carrier request (here: DHL demo), pulls a label, and pushes status + tracking back into the same spreadsheet row. Your team does not re-type tracking into chat or mail—the sheet becomes the source of truth through delivered.
