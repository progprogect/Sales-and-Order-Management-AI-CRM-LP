Frequently Asked Questions

Does the AI really understand my product nuances? expand_more

Yes. We train the model on your actual chat history, product descriptions, and policies. It can handle returns, sizing advice, and technical specs.

Can I step in if the AI makes a mistake? expand_more

Absolutely. The unified inbox shows live conversations. Managers can override the AI at any moment with a single click.

How does the one-time payment work? expand_more

We believe in ownership. You pay for the implementation and license once. You only pay for your own AI usage (OpenAI API) at cost price.

Which social networks are supported? expand_more

Instagram, Telegram, WhatsApp, Viber, and Facebook Messenger are all supported out of the box.

What if we use a different shipping provider? expand_more

We prioritize Belpost and Europost for deep automation, but custom integrations for others are possible as a custom add-on.
